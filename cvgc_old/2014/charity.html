2014 CVGC - Charity Info

I'm not going to ramble on. If you want to review the charities from previous years, then check out the Historical Page.

In 2012 we raised hundreds of dollars for cancer patients at MD Anderson in the Woodlands.
In 2013 we raised over $600 for a young girl who had been diagnosed with leukaemia.
For 2014, let's see if we can break $1000!
All I ask is that, in order to attend this event (whether you're in the tournament or not) you meet one of the following criteria:

  1. You must bring an Xbox 360, Wii, or PS3 game (or more than one if you'd like) to be donated to the Texas Children's Hospital of Houston. All games should be rated E or lower. I can't imagine that they'd take a donation of your old copy of Grand Theft Auto. Sorry!
  2. You must purchase something off of the Child's Play Charity Amazon Wish List for the Texas Children's Hospital of Houston.
  3. You must bring at least $20. The recipient of this money for 2014 will be the 11q Research and Resources Group. Please donate what you can, and remember that every little bit helps. If you want to donate cash on TOP of another donation, then you'll earn extra karma points and maybe a special something from big D himself. Don't worry, you'll get enough free beer and snacks that you won't be losing out.
I still have some left over koozies from 2012. I will sell them $5 each with ALL money going to the kitty.


Again, ALL money collected will go directly to our charity, NOT just the proceeds.
Everyone is also free to bring their own items to donate for auction/sale. Examples include games, art, movies, whatever.
In other words, there is a possibility that some other stuff will be auctioned off, so please come with generous wallets and remember how fortunate we all are to be here playing games instead of worrying about stuff that really matters.
