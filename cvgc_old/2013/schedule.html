2013 CVGC - Schedule

I'm going to keep this page simple.
Ultimately, this is your weekend.
The following schedule is a loose suggesion of what I think we should do. It's open for debate and will probably be adjusted on the fly.

Friday

People will be trickling in throughout the night so we'll just sit around, talk, eat, game (board, card, video, whatever), watch movies, etc. etc. Definitely plan on another round of Cards Against Humanity. If anyone has any other big "party" style games, this would be a good time to break them out.

Saturday

AM:
Get up and eat sugar filled cereals and watch a few old school cartoons.
I'm guessing we can watch such classics as TMNT and Jem
LATE AM/EARLY PM:
Get started on the gaming. The past two years I have tried to organize trips and such...but NOT this year. We're just going to hang out and have fun. No pressure, no real structure. If you want to hold a tournament, do it. If you want to convince a few people to run to the store with you, great. If you just want to walk around and watch others play and cheer them on, you have my support.
PM:
CVGC 2013
Late PM:
BACK TO THE GAMES! CRANK UP THE MUSIC! PARTY UNTIL YOUR FACE MELTS!
If you're planning on staying at my place for the night, that's fine, but with that you have to understand that some people are going to stay up and I do NOT expect them to be quiet (within reason). The point of this night is a party and that's what we're going to do. No hard feelings, just warning you.

Sunday

AM:
Take it easy. Probably watch a movie or something. Go back to gaming if you're h@rdc0r.
