2013 CVGC - Charity Info

I'm not going to ramble on as much as last year. If you want more background on why this is important to me, I suggest that you check out last year's charity page.

Last year we raised hundreds of dollars for cancer patients at MD Anderson in the Woodlands. Let's see if we can break $1000 this year!
All I ask is that, in order to attend this event (whether you're in the tournament or not) you meet one of the following criteria:

  1. You must bring an Xbox 360, Wii, or PS3 game (or more than one if you'd like) to be donated to the Texas Children's Hospital of Houston. All games should be rated E or lower. I can't imagine that they'd take a donation of your old copy of Grand Theft Auto. Sorry!
  2. You must purchase something off of the Child's Play Charity Amazon Wish List for the Texas Children's Hospital of Houston.
  3. You must bring $20. ALL of this fee will be donated to MD Anderson specifically to provide financial support to cancer patients that need help but make too much money to be elligable for governmental support. Don't worry, you'll get enough free beer and snacks that you won't be losing out.
I still have some left over koozies from last year. I will sell them $5 each with ALL money going to the kitty for MD Anderson.


Last year I auctioned off a complete N64 set. Unfortunately it doesn't look like I'll be able to top that this year, but I'm keeping an eye out for something. Expect there to be various things for auction/sale. Again, ALL money collected will go directly to MD Anderson, NOT just the proceeds. If anyone has any suggestions for good items, please let me know.
Everyone is also free to bring their own items to donate for auction/sale. Examples include games, art, movies, whatever.
In other words, there is a possibility that some other stuff will be auctioned off, so please come with generous wallets and remember how fortunate we all are to be here playing games instead of worrying about stuff that really matters.
