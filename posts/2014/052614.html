The Finals

  1. 60 Game Marathon

    Platform: Various
    Scoring: One Point for EVERY Win
    Details: Some of you might have played this once before. The glorious part about this is that it doesn't matter. For those in the dark, I whipped up a script that will randomly cycle through 60 games in 1 minute intervals. The object is to best your opponent in each round. The top score each round earns a point. The top two people heading into the final round will serve as team captains. Each person will pick their team from the rest of the players. What are you playing for if you're not one of the top two peeps? Pride. That's what.
    Tips: Haha. HAHA. HAHAHWWWAHHAHAAHA. Be in first or second and then don't pick any chumps. :-P
