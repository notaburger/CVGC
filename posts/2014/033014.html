Site Overhauled (yet again) The date has been set. Mark your calendars. Tell your loved ones. Ask off from work. CVGC TWO THOUSAND AND FOURTEEN IS ALMOST HERE!

So, we shouldn't have any more issues with the website going down. I finally decided to stop being a cheap skate and sprung for real hosting through amazon. I know that everyone is relieved.

Erica has set up a Facebook event page to help keep everyone up to date. All it's really going to do is let you all know when the website is updated. If you want to know the latest, keep it locked here.

The only major thing left to decide is our games list for this year. If you have any personal recommendations, please send them my way. I intend to post the final game list a week early this year just so that I'm not scrambling at the last minute. I'm sorry if that removes the mystique of it all, but it'll help me keep my sanity. If you want to be surprised, then just skip that portion of the site.

We're going to be LOADED with bevs for 2014. I've got 5 gallons of Warp Whistle already on tap, 13 gallons of Red Overhead fermenting, 5 gallons of Apple Cider aging, and 5 gallons each of Root Beer and "Old Fashioned" Cream Soda carbing. Plus, a little birdy told me that someone might be bringing some extra homebrew with them as well. If we run out of drinks, then I will un-friend each and every one of you as you are all raging alcoholics.
...nah, I'll probably just go pick up some PBR.
