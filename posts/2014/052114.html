big D's Classic Video Game Challenge

MAY 21

I TOLD you to believe Lookie here! I actually followed through! You know what to do.

The Games

  1. Snow Bros.

    Platform: THE Nintendo Entertainment System
    Scoring: Top Score, No Continues, Co-op
    Details: This game is basically Bubble Bobble with snow. The scoring for this one is going to be identical to the way that we scored Bubble Bobble a few years back. Your team's total score will determine your rank and then your personal score will determine who gets the higher points. In other words, the first team will get the top two scores with the highest single player getting the top honors. The team with the second highest score will get 3rd and 4th place, the next will get 5th and 6th, and so on and so forth (So yes, it is possible that the person in second will have the absolute worst score of anyone. The name of the game is teamwork!).
    Tips: Work together as a team, but don't forget that the rest of the night you're flying solo.

  2. Wave Race

    Platform: N64
    Scoring: Fastest Time, Track TBD
    Details: This was a really hard choice. This one made the cut at the expense of other N64 classics such as GoldenEye, Star Fox, Mario Kart, and Smash Bros. Wave Race deserves its chance to shine though. Would it have been popular if there were more than 5 games available for the N64? Probably not, but it's safely the best water based racing game ever created. The wave physics and music still stand up even today.
    Tips: You have to watch how you catch the waves. Timing is everything. It's like Excitebike except that the ramps move on you!

  3. Galaga

    Platform: Arcade
    Scoring: Top Score, One Quarter
    Details: Um...it's Galaga. You know? Pew, pew? Yeah. I knew that you knew. This one was a special request. After this CVGC, it's going to have to go back into the vault for a few years to give other games a chance, but, for now, let's party.
    Tips: You have to intentionally get your fighter captured so that you can rescue it and double up your fire power...or I could be lying to you and that could be a TERRIBLE idea. Hmmmmmm.

I still have one final game to announce. We're doing something different this year, but that's the only tease you're getting now. I'll spill the beans here on Sunday.
