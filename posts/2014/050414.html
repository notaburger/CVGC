big D's Classic Video Game Challenge

MAY 4

If you miss this event, you're nothing but a scruffy looking nerf herder! It's finally time to start revealing the games. I'm announcing the first 3 today, and I plan to post the final 3 by next week. Without any further ado, let's get to brass tacks.

The Games

  1. Paperboy

    Platform: NES
    Scoring: Top Score, No Continues
    Details: How have we gone so many years without playing Paperboy? Honestly, I feel like I've dropped the ball as a host. Hopefully all will be forgiven this year as we finally have our chance to see who can run the best route. Also, did you know that Paperboy was the first NES game developed in the USA? USA! USA! USA! Now, someone needs to tell me why the kid on the cover is 80 years old.
    Tips: Check out this page for tips. Pay special attention to the Points and Scoring section. Whatever you do, don't click on this link or the music will forever haunt your dreams.

  2. Joust

    Platform: Arcade
    Scoring: 1v1, Single Elimination Tournament, Score Based on Round Eliminated
    Details: Joust is kind of a perfect arcade game. It combines equal parts collaboration and competition. You never feel betrayed by the machine...always by your comrade.
    Tips: You can always look into gameplay videos, such as this one, but you'll always be better off practising. My advice? Go for the other player first. It's hard to score points when you're dead.

  3. Chu Chu Rocket

    Platform: Dreamcast
    Scoring: 1v1v1v1, First Round Determines Seed, Second Round Determines Score
    Details: So here we have our first "cooky" game for 2014. Chu Chu Rocket is a game that doesn't get much respect in this day and age. It's kind of like Krusty's Fun House, but a LOT more frantic. Is the Dreamcast considered "classic"? I say so...plus, it's a hoot and a half.
    Tips: Yeah.....this isn't going to help. Just breathe slowly.

That's all for today. What do you guys think so far? Are we missing your favorite game? Do you hate any of the choices? It's not like it's going to change anything, but feel free to vent in the comments section below!
