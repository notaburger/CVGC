big D's Classic Video Game Challenge

JAN 24

Things are moving right along! The Warp Whistle was just kegged tonight. Do you know what that means? It means CVGC is RIGHT around the corner!

So, what are the plans for this year you ask? Well, last year I tried to expand the tournament to more players and I realized, yet again, that most people would rather just show up, drink some beer, and casually play some games (and, honestly, I can't blame them)...so that's what we're going to do...sort of. Since there are a few people that still cherish the competition, I'm moving the tourney to Friday and moving the crazy party/fund raising event to Saturday. If you don't get invited to the tournament on Friday, don't fret. Just let me know and I'll pencil you in.

Now that that's out of the way, let's talk more specific details.

Last year was a fantastic success as a fund raiser. We raised over $600 for a young girl in need. See below for the card that she wrote for us.





Our goal this year is to break through the $1000 barrier. This year we are going to be raising funds for Jacobsen's Syndrome research. The previously linked website does a much better job explaining the disorder than I could, so please check it out. Erica and I are honored to be able to support such a worth charity.
